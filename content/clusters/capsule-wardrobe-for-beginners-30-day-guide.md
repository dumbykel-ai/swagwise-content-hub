## Week 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)

### Day 1: Set Your Target (15 minutes)

**Action:** Decide your capsule size based on lifestyle.

| Lifestyle | Recommended Size |
|-----------|------------------|
| Simple (WFH, casual) | 30-35 items |
| Moderate (office + social) | 35-45 items |
| Complex (multiple dress codes) | 45-55 items |

**Write it down:** "My target capsule size is ___ items."

**Why this matters:** A clear target guides every subsequent decision.

### Day 2: Identify Your Style DNA (20 minutes)

**Action:** Pull out your 10 most-worn items. Examine them.

**Note:**
- What colors dominate?
- What silhouettes repeat?
- What style categories appear? (casual, classic, edgy, romantic)
- How do these items make you feel?

**Write it down:** "My Style DNA is: [colors], [silhouettes], [style category]."

**Example:** "My Style DNA is: Navy/gray/white, structured/fitted, classic minimalist."

### Day 3: Define Your Color Palette (15 minutes)

**Action:** Using the 60-30-10 rule, define your colors.

- **60% Base (neutrals):** Pick 2-3 neutral colors from your most-worn items
- **30% Accent:** Pick 2 colors that complement your bases
- **10% Pop:** Pick 1 bold color for statements

**Write it down:** "My palette is: Base [___], Accent [___], Pop [___]."

**Example:** "Base: Navy, gray, white. Accent: Burgundy, camel. Pop: Red."

### Day 4: Map Your Lifestyle Needs (15 minutes)

**Action:** List your weekly activities and contexts.

**Example:**
- Work: 5 days (business casual)
- Casual: 2 days (weekends)
- Exercise: 4x/week (excluded from capsule)
- Social: 1-2 events (elevated casual)
- Formal: 1x/month (dressy)

**Write it down:** "I need clothes for: [list contexts]."

### Day 5: Category Planning (20 minutes)

**Action:** Allocate your target number across categories.

**For 40-item capsule:**
| Category | Count |
|----------|-------|
| Tops | 12 |
| Bottoms | 8 |
| Dresses | 4 |
| Outerwear | 6 |
| Shoes | 6 |
| Bags | 4 |

**Adjust for your life:** More bottoms if you don't wear dresses. More outerwear if you have cold climate. Make it yours.

### Day 6: Set Up Tracking (15 minutes)

**Action:** Download Swagwise (or create a spreadsheet) to track your wardrobe.

**Why this matters:** You need visibility into what you own and what you wear. Guessing doesn't work.

### Day 7: Rest and Review (10 minutes)

**Action:** Review your Week 1 decisions:
- Target size: ___
- Style DNA: ___
- Color palette: ___
- Lifestyle needs: ___
- Category allocation: ___

**Adjust anything that feels wrong.** This foundation guides everything else.

## Week 2: Audit (Days 8-14)

### Day 8: Photograph Tops (30 minutes)

**Action:** Pull out every top you own. Photograph each one.

Upload to Swagwise or add to your tracking system. Don't judge yetâ€”just document.

**Tip:** Laid flat on bed with good lighting works well. Quick photos are fine.

### Day 9: Photograph Bottoms (20 minutes)

**Action:** Same process for all pants, skirts, shorts.

### Day 10: Photograph Dresses/Outerwear (25 minutes)

**Action:** Document dresses, jumpsuits, blazers, jackets, coats.

### Day 11: Photograph Shoes/Bags (20 minutes)

**Action:** Complete your catalog with shoes and bags.

**Milestone:** You now have visibility into your ENTIRE wardrobe. For most people, this is the first time ever seeing everything at once.

### Day 12: Mark Your "Always Wear" Items (20 minutes)

**Action:** Go through your catalog. Star/mark items you wear constantly.

These are your capsule core. You probably have 15-25 items in this category.

### Day 13: Mark Your "Never Wear" Items (20 minutes)

**Action:** Identify items you haven't worn in 6+ months.

Be honest. Items you're "saving for someday" count as never-wear if someday hasn't come in 6 months.

### Day 14: Calculate Your Gap (15 minutes)

**Action:** Compare your "always wear" count to your target.

- "Always wear" items: ___
- Target capsule size: ___
- Gap to fill: ___

**Example:** 18 always-wear items, 40 target = 22 more items to select from the "middle" pile.

---

## Week 3: Curate (Days 15-21)

### Day 15: Evaluate "Sometimes Wear" Tops (25 minutes)

**Action:** For each top not in "always" or "never" category, ask:

1. Does it fit my Style DNA? (color, silhouette, style)
2. Does it coordinate with 3+ items in my "always wear" pile?
3. Does it fill a lifestyle need?
4. Do I feel good wearing it?

**4 yes = Keep. 3 yes = Maybe. 2 or fewer = Remove pile.**

### Day 16: Evaluate "Sometimes Wear" Bottoms (20 minutes)

**Action:** Same evaluation process for bottoms.

### Day 17: Evaluate Dresses/Outerwear (25 minutes)

**Action:** Same evaluation process for dresses, blazers, jackets, coats.

### Day 18: Evaluate Shoes/Bags (20 minutes)

**Action:** Complete evaluation for accessories.

### Day 19: Count and Adjust (20 minutes)

**Action:** Count your "Keep" items across all categories.

- If under target: Great! You have room for strategic additions.
- If at target: Perfect! Move to next phase.
- If over target: Re-evaluate "maybe" items more strictly.

### Day 20: Check Category Balance (15 minutes)

**Action:** Compare your keeps to your category plan.

Are you heavy on tops but light on bottoms? Missing shoes for certain contexts? Note the gaps.

### Day 21: Check Color Balance (15 minutes)

**Action:** Verify your keeps follow 60-30-10.

Approximately:
- 60% in base colors?
- 30% in accent colors?
- 10% in pop colors?

If unbalanced, note which colors are missing.

---

## Week 4: Complete (Days 22-30)

### Day 22: Box the Removes (30 minutes)

**Action:** Pack all "remove" items into boxes. Label with today's date.

**Store for 3 months.** If you don't retrieve anything, donate the box unopened.

**Why not discard immediately:** This creates a safety net, reducing fear and enabling bolder decisions.

### Day 23: Organize Your Capsule (30 minutes)

**Action:** Reorganize your closet to feature ONLY your capsule items.

- Move capsule items to prime positions
- Store off-season items separately
- Create visibility (front-facing, not crammed)

### Day 24: Create 10 Outfits (25 minutes)

**Action:** Using only your capsule, create and photograph 10 complete outfits.

This proves your capsule works and creates a reference library for busy mornings.

### Day 25: Identify Gaps (20 minutes)

**Action:** Based on outfit creation, what's missing?

- Couldn't create work outfit? Gap: professional piece
- No outfit for evening event? Gap: elevated item
- Same pants in every outfit? Gap: bottoms variety

**Create specific shopping list:** "Navy blazer, structured, size M" not "something for work."

### Day 26: Test Day 1 (Track normally)

**Action:** Live with your capsule. Wear only capsule items. Note any friction.

### Day 27: Test Day 2 (Track normally)

**Action:** Continue testing. What works? What's missing?

### Day 28: Test Day 3 (Track normally)

**Action:** Three days provides meaningful data on capsule functionality.

### Day 29: Adjust Based on Testing (20 minutes)

**Action:** Review your test days.

- Any gaps that prevented good outfits?
- Any items you didn't reach for even once?
- Any contexts you couldn't dress for?

Make notes for future refinement.

### Day 30: Celebrate and Plan (15 minutes)

**Action:** You did it. You have a functioning capsule wardrobe.

**Next steps:**
- Fill identified gaps strategically (not impulsively)
- Continue tracking what you wear
- Schedule 90-day review to assess and adjust
